Next Steps for All Applicants

All applicants must complete and clear the following before beginning volunteer service:

  • Application for Volunteer Services
  • Background Screening
  • Drug Screening
  • HMC Employee Health (Vaccinations w/ Employee Health Nurse)
  • New Volunteer Orientation

Once all requirements are cleared through Human Resources and the Education Department, and after attending New Volunteer Orientation (you will be notified of the date and time), you will be assigned an area of service and a schedule.

If you have any questions regarding your application or the volunteer process, please contact our Executive Assistant at 956-365-1027.

Thank you for choosing Harlingen Medical Center for your volunteer service. We look forward to welcoming you to our team.
